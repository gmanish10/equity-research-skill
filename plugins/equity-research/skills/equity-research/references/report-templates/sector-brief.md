# Sector brief — deliverable template

Concise (1–2 pages). Designed for chat by default; structured so it can be written to docx if requested.

## Structure

### Header
- `{Sector Name} — Sector Brief`
- `{Date}`
- One-line view (top-right): e.g., "OVERWEIGHT — Semi-conductor strength drives sector leadership through 2026"

### 1. Sector composition
- Sub-industries (from `get_sector_data`)
- Top 8–10 names by market cap with quick stats
- Top ETFs (flagship cap-weighted + equal-weight alt + thematic)

### 2. Sector aggregates
- Weighted P/E, P/S, EV/EBITDA (from top 10)
- vs. 5Y history
- Earnings revision direction (aggregate from key names)
- Sector ETF performance vs. SPY (1M / 3M / YTD / 1Y)

### 3. Macro regime fit
- What regime favors this sector?
- Current regime match
- Rotation signals

### 4. Best-in-class / worst-in-class
Peer table sorted by composite score:
| Ticker | Market cap | Rev growth | Op margin | ROIC | Fwd P/E | 6M rel perf | Score |

### 5. Entry vehicles
Ranked by conviction + risk level:
- Flagship ETF
- Equal-weight alternative
- Thematic / narrower ETF
- Best single-name bet
- Leveraged ETF (if aggressive sizing warrants)

### 6. Aggressive-lens verdict
```
Sector view:     [Overweight / Neutral / Underweight]
Conviction:      [High / Medium / Low]
Best vehicle:    [ticker]
Position size:   [X% of portfolio]
Time horizon:    [ST / MT / LT emphasis]
WHAT KILLS THIS CALL: [specific triggers]
Invalidation:    [sector ETF weekly close below $X]
```
